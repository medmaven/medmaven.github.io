Account Deletion Policy

We value your privacy and strive to make it easy for you to manage your account data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so by submitting a request.

To delete your account, please send an email to the following address:

medmaven4@gmail.com

In your email, please include the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address. Once we receive your request, we will process the account deletion and notify you upon completion. Please allow up to 7 business days for your request to be processed.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding your account deletion, feel free to contact us at the email address above.
